Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Hack The Blob?

Hack The Blob is a hackathon

Who can participate?

We welcome anyone, regardless of age! Whether you're a middle schooler or university student, you can participate in Hack The Blob.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

Make sure to bring your laptop and a charger. The event will be a day long, so feel free to bring whatever makes you comfortable!

How many people can I have on a team?

You may compete in groups of at most 4 members. It is recommended that you form your team prior to the event.

Can I participate even if I don't know how to code?

Absolutely! Beginners are always welcome. There will be workshops dedicated to help you learn how to create your product.

How much will it cost to attend?

Approximately $0. Anyone may attend Hack The Blob for free!

I have a question that hasn't been answered here. Where can I ask?

You can contact us on our Discord server, our Twitter account at @HackTheBlob, or send us an email.
